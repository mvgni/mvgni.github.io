About Me

My name is Connor Thorson. I am a UI designer and front-end web developer currently living in Corvallis, Oregon. I was raised in West Des Moines, Iowa and graduated from the University of Northern Iowa with a BA in Interactive Digital Studies, and with emphases in Computer Science and Visualization.

I currently work as a web content manager at Mindz Eye Marketing; a marketing company based in Des Moines, Iowa. I also work as a contracted web developer at the Environmental Protection Agency.

Feel free to check out my work or say hi!
